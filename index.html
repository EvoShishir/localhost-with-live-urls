Our Fast & Effective Water Extraction and Removal Process

Time is of the essence when dealing with water damage. The longer water sits, the greater the risk of mold growth, structural damage, and other problems. That's why our team at Dalworth Restoration acts swiftly. We follow a proven cleanup process to effectively restore your property while minimizing further damage and giving you peace of mind. Our water extraction process involves:

Step 1
Inspection

Our team performs a thorough inspection to assess the level of the water damage and identify any potential hazards.

Step 2
Water Categorization

We make sure to categorize the water based on its contamination level to determine the best water extraction method.

Step 3
Water Extraction

Using advanced equipment, our team quickly removes all standing water to minimize further damage to your property.

Step 4
Drying & Dehumidification

Industrial-grade fans and dehumidifiers are used to dry the affected areas thoroughly and completely eliminate moisture.

Step 5
Cleaning & Disinfecting

We thoroughly clean and disinfect all surfaces to remove contaminants and prevent the growth of mold, mildew or bacteria.

Step 6
Repair & Restoration

Our restoration crew repairs and restores any structural damage, returning your property to its pre-damage condition.
