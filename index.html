Traditional Rugs

If you're drawn to the rich history of rug weaving, our traditional rugs will feel right at home under your feet. These hand-knotted pieces feature classic motifs and symmetrical patterns inspired by centuries-old designs from Persia, Turkey, India, and beyond.

With deep, natural dyes and intricate borders, traditional rugs bring warmth and character to formal living rooms, dining areas, and libraries. From Heriz to Tabriz, we carry a wide range of authentic styles that deliver both comfort and culture.

Transitional Rugs

For those who enjoy a mix of old and new, our transitional rugs offer the best of both worlds. These rugs pair the classic motifs of traditional pieces with a modern twist, muted palettes, minimal patterns, or distressed finishes.

They're perfect for homes that combine vintage charm with a contemporary edge. Transitional rugs work beautifully in family rooms, open-plan spaces, or anywhere you want a little harmony between eras.

European Rugs

Our European rug collection brings a touch of sophistication to any interior. These finely crafted pieces often feature elegant florals, soft pastels, and symmetrical borders that reflect centuries of European artistry.

From French Aubusson to Savonnerie designs, these rugs are ideal for formal rooms, historic homes, or any setting where refinement is key. Each one is handpicked for quality and grace, offering a classic European look with lasting appeal.
